Installing the Junie Plugin

To get started, you need to install the Junie plugin from the JetBrains Marketplace. Junie is available for all modern JetBrains IDEs, including IntelliJ IDEA, PyCharm, WebStorm, and others.

The process is straightforward:

  1. Open your IDE's settings (Ctrl+Alt+S or File â†’ Settings).
  2. Navigate to the Plugins section.
  3. Make sure you are on the Marketplace tab.
  4. In the search bar, type "Junie" and press Enter.
  5. Find the "JetBrains Junie" plugin and click Install.
  6. After the installation is complete, you will be prompted to restart your IDE. Please do so.

To use Junie, you need an active JetBrains AI subscription linked to your JetBrains account. Make sure you are logged into your account in the IDE.
Accessing Junie

Once your IDE has restarted, you will see a new Junie icon in the right-hand sidebar. Clicking it will open the Junie tool window, which is your main interface for interacting with the agent.

Let's make sure everything is set up correctly.

  • Follow the steps above to install the plugin and restart your IDE.
  • Ensure you are logged in to the account with an active AI subscription. You can check this in the top-right corner of your IDE.
  • Click the Junie icon on the right sidebar to open the chat panel.
